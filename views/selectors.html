Use the Tutor For a Card function to search the entire database of Magic cards to search for a card whose name includes the search parameters. When finding a card you like, click on the name to read more and click the Add to Deck button to add to your cardlist.

Access your current decklist by clicking on Research For a Library. Note: as of the current iteration of the site, attempting to add the same card more than once to the decklist causes the decklist to delete itself.
